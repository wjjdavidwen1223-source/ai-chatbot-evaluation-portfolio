# Case Study 1: Naturalness & Redundancy

## Test Goal
Evaluate whether the sentence demonstrates natural, fluent, and concise Chinese expression.

## Original Sentence
我很喜欢学习中文，因为它让我觉得很有意思而且可以让我认识很多新的朋友们。

**Error Type:** Naturalness; Redundancy

## Issue Identified
- Redundant phrasing (“让我觉得很有意思”)
- Repetition in “新的朋友们”
- Slightly unnatural sentence structure

## Improved Version
我很喜欢学习中文，因为它很有意思，还能让我认识很多新朋友。

## Analysis
The original sentence is grammatically understandable but lacks natural fluency. The phrase “让我觉得很有意思” is redundant and can be simplified.

Additionally, “新的朋友们” introduces duplication, as “新朋友” already implies plurality and novelty.

This reflects a common issue in AI-generated or learner Chinese: redundancy and overly literal expression.
