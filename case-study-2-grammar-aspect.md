# Case Study 2: Grammar Error & Aspect Misuse

## Test Goal
Evaluate correct usage of aspect markers and grammatical structure.

## Original Sentence
如果我有时间的话，我会去看电影了。

## Issue Identified
- Incorrect use of “了”
- Conflict between future intention and completion marker
- Unnatural conditional structure

## Improved Version
如果我有时间的话，我会去看电影。

## Analysis
The sentence incorrectly uses the aspect marker “了,” which typically indicates completed actions.

Here, the sentence expresses future intention, making “了” inappropriate.

This reflects a common learner and AI error: misuse of aspect markers in future or conditional contexts.
